Overview

RAYMOND TEN-X HABITAT

O.C. Received 2 years ahead of the Rear Timeline for Towers A, B & C Ten X HABITAT stands as a flourishing gated community, elevating the bar for superior living standards. It's a meticulous blend of sustainability, modern architectural marvels, and contemporary aesthetics. Situated at the heart of Pokhran Road in Thane, it's strategically placed near top-tier schools, hospitals, dining spots, shopping centres, and more.

Ten X HABITAT isn't just a development; it embodies a vision for the future of living, promising a serene, hassle-free, and cozy lifestyle. Every amenity and service offered goes beyond the ordinary, enhancing your living experience significantly.
